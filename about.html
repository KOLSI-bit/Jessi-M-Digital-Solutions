About Us

Your Growth Partner — Not Just Another Marketing Agency

At Jessi M Digital Solutions, we are a digital growth consultancy dedicated to helping businesses attract more customers, generate consistent enquiries, and scale with confidence. We design, build, and manage complete customer acquisition systems that combine powerful advertising, strategic social media management, high-quality content creation, and intelligent automation.

What We Do Best

  • Strategic Facebook & Google Advertising
  • Professional Social Media Management
  • Content Shooting & Professional Editing
  • UGC (User-Generated Content) Creation
  • WhatsApp Lead Generation & Automation
  • CRM Setup and Lead Management

Our mission

Help businesses stop chasing customers and start attracting them through tailored growth strategies that deliver measurable results — more enquiries, more bookings, more calls, and more sales.

Our Process

  1. Business Discovery — We learn about your business, goals, and target customers.
  2. Customer Growth Strategy — We develop a customised strategy for your audience.
  3. Campaign Launch — High-converting ads and content to attract enquiries.
  4. Lead Management — Enquiries captured via WhatsApp, CRM, or landing pages.
  5. Continuous Optimisation — Monitor, test, and refine campaigns for performance.
  6. Growth Reporting — Clear reports with performance and next steps.
