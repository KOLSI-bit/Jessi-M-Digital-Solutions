Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

How long before I start seeing results?

Many clients begin receiving enquiries within the first few weeks, though timelines vary depending on your industry, competition, and advertising budget.

Do you guarantee sales?

We focus on generating qualified enquiries. We cannot guarantee sales as factors like your pricing, product quality, and sales process are outside our control.

Do I need a website?

Not necessarily. We can generate leads through WhatsApp, Meta Instant Forms, or dedicated landing pages.

Is advertising spend included?

No. Your advertising budget is paid directly to Facebook or Google for full transparency and control.

Do you work with businesses internationally?

Yes. We work remotely with businesses worldwide and support clients via online meetings, phone, email, and WhatsApp.
